Fubini's Theorem




Main results

  • $\displaystyle \int_a^b F'(x)\,dx =F(b)-F(a)$
  • $\displaystyle \int\limits_C \nabla f \cdot d\r =f\left(\r(b)\right)-f\left(\r(a)\right)$
  • $\displaystyle \iint\limits_D\left( \frac{\partial F_2}{\partial x}- \frac{\partial F_1}{\partial y} \right) \,dA = \int\limits_C F_1\, dx + F_2 \,dy$
  • $\displaystyle \iint\limits_S \curl \F \cdot dS = \int\limits_C \F \cdot d\r$
  • $\displaystyle \iiint\limits_E \div \F \, dV = \iint\limits_S \F \cdot \n\, dS $
