Example 1: $\displaystyle\lim_{x\ra 5}\left(x^2-3x\right)=10$

Proof: Let $\epsilon>0$. Choose \[ \delta =\min\left(3, \frac{\epsilon}{10}\right) \] such that $x\in \R\setminus\{5\}$ and $\abs{x-5}\lt \delta$, implies that \[ |x^2-3x-10|=|x+2||x-5|\lt 10|x-5|\lt 10 \frac{\epsilon}{10}=\epsilon. \;\;\blacksquare \]

Example 3: $\displaystyle\lim_{x\ra 7}\left(x^2+1\right)=50$
