BROWSERS:

Please use Chrome, Firefox or Safari.

KEY CODES:

Press 'B' to toggle chalkboard.
Press 'C' to toggle notes.
Press 'X' to cycle colors forward.
Press 'Y' to cycle colors backward.

Press 'DEL' to clear chalkboard.
Press 'BACKSPACE' to reset all chalkboard.

Press '?' for other keyboard shortcuts.

PDF EXPORT:

Slides format - To download a PDF file of the slides, just open this LINK in Google Chrome and use the print dialog (this may not work for other browsers and the format may vary).

Unformatted - To download as a PDF file from the menu of your browser select 'Print' and then select the option 'Save as PDF'.
