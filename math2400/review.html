Topics for the final exam

  • Definition of supremum and infimum: Algebraic properties.
  • Convergence of sequences: $\varepsilon$-$N$ proofs.
  • Convergence tests for series.
  • Limits of functions: $\varepsilon$-$\delta$ proofs.
  • Uniform and Lipschitz continuity.
  • Riemann integration: Criterion of integrability, lower and upper sums, partitions.
  • Sequences of functions: Pointwise Convergence, Uniform convergence using uniform norm, interchange of limits.
  • Partial derivatives, directional derivatives and differentiability of functions $f:\mathbb R^2\to \mathbb R$.
  • Inverse function theorem in $\R^n$.
