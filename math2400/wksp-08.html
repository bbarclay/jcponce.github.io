Problem 1

(a) $\dfrac{d}{dx}\left(\ds\int_{-x}^{x}e^{s^2}\, ds\right),$        (b) $\dfrac{d}{dx}\left(\ds\int_{0}^{x^2}\sin \left(s^2\right)\, ds\right).$








Problem 2

Suppose $f:[a,b]\ra \R$ is continuous and $\int_a^xf=\int_x^bf$ for all $x\in[a,b]$. Show that $f(x)=0$ for all $x\in[a,b]$.








Problem 3

Suppose $f:[a,b]\ra \R$ is continuous and $\int_a^xf=0$ for all irrational $x\in[a,b]$. Show that $f(x)=0$ for all $x\in[a,b]$.
