Problem 1

Find and prove the limits:

(a) $\displaystyle \lim_{x\to c} \sqrt{x} $, for $c \geq 0$     (b) $\displaystyle \lim_{x\to 0} \sin\left(\frac{1}{x^2}\right) $     (c) $\displaystyle \lim_{x\to 0} x^2\cos\left(\frac{1}{x}\right) $








Problem 2

Let $f \colon \R \to \R$ be defined by setting $f(x):=x$ if $x$ is rational and $f(x):=0$ if $x$ is irrational. Show that $ \lim_{x\ra c}f(x)$ exist if $c=0$; and does not exists if $c\neq 0 $.








Problem 7

Prove that $\displaystyle \lim_{x \to \infty} f(x)=L$, with $L\in \R$, is unique if it exists.
