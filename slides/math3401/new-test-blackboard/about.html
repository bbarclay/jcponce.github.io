Instructions:

Press '?' for keyboard shortcuts To obtain a PDF-file of the slides open this link in Google Chrome and use the print dialog (this may not work for other browsers).

To download as a single PDF-file just print it from your browser and save it as PDF.

Key codes:

Press 'B' to toggle chalkboard.
Press 'C' to toggle notes.
Press 'X' to cycle colors forward.
Press 'Y' to cycle colors backward.
Press 'BACKSPACE' to reset all chalkboard.
Press 'DEL' to clear chalkboard.
Press 'D' to download chalkboard drawing.
