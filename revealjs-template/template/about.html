KEY CODES:

Press 'B' to toggle chalkboard.
Press 'C' to toggle notes.
Press 'X' to cycle colors forward.
Press 'Y' to cycle colors backward.
Press 'BACKSPACE' to reset all chalkboard.
Press 'DEL' to clear chalkboard.

Press '?' for other keyboard shortcuts.

PDF EXPORT:

To download as a PDF file (with no fragments) from the menu of your browser select 'Print' and then select the option 'Save as PDF'.

If you prefer a PDF file of the slides with fragments, just open this LINK in Google Chrome and use the print dialog (this may not work for other browsers).
